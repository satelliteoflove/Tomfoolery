MAGE SPELLS
===========
Level 1
-------
Katino (Sleep) - During battle
Put one group (slot) of enemies to sleep. The damage of direct attack is doubled for sleeping enemies.

Halito (Flame) - During battle
1-8 dmg to one enemy (flame)

Dumapic (Map Pic) - In Camp
Show auto map

Mogref (Shield) - During battle
Decrease caster's AC by 2

Level 2
-------
Melito (Flame2) - During battle
1-8 dmg to one group (slot) of enemies (flame)

Sogref (Guard) - During battle
Decrease AC of one ally by 2

Dilto (?) - During battle
Increase AC of 1 group (slot) of enemies by 2

Bolatu (Petrify) - During battle
Petrify one enemy for the duration of the battle

Level 3
-------
Morlis (Fear) - During battle
Stop the actions of one group (slot) of enemies and increase their AC by 4.

Mahalito (Flame3) - During battle
4-24 dmg to one group (slot) of enemies (flame)

Cortu (Magic Guard) - During battle
Strengthen barriers against enemy spells and breath

Kantios (Silence) - During battle
Suppress spells, breaths, summoning actions of one group of enemies

Level 4
-------
Tzalik (Shock) - During battle
24 to 48 damage to a single enemy

Lahalito (Flame4) - During battle
6-36 damage (flame) to one group (slot) of enemies

Litofeit (Float) - In camp
Floats in the air to avoid traps and shoots. It gets harder to preempt (? - maybe talking about "surprising the enemy")

Rokdo (Petrify2) - In battle
Stop the action of one group (slot) of enemies (petrification)

Level 5
-------
Socordi (Summon) - In battle
Summon one type at random from the following monsters
・Smoke daemon
・Succubus
・Seidel
・Gargoyle
・Flack
・Siphon
・Andrea Luffs

Madalto (Freeze) - In battle
8-64 damage to one group (slot) of enemies (cold)

Palios (Cancel) - In battle or camp
Cancels out magical energies - in camp, all status effects are removed. In battle, erase the effects of Bacortu (seal) and Cortu (Magic Guard).

Bacortu (Silence) - Seal the spells of one group (slot) of enemies.

Level 6
-------
Zilwan (Dispel) - In battle
Deals 1000-10000 dmg to a single undead enemy

Ladalto (Frost2) - In battle
Deals 10-100 dmg (cold) to one group (slot) of enemies.
