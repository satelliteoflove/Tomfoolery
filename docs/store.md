Store menu:
- Buy
- Sell
- Uncurse
- Identify
- Pool Gold
- Done

Definitions:
Buy:
    Buy items from the merchant.

Sell:
    Sell items to the merchant.

Uncurse:
    Remove a cursed, equipped item from a character. Item is destroyed in the
    process.

Identify:
    Identify general properties of item, including name and specific type.
    Traits such as alignment and/or curse mechanisms are not disclosed.

Pool Gold:
    Current character receives all gold from all current party members.

Done:
    Exit store.
